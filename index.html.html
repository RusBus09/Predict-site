FAQ

Common Questions

Do streamers need to set anything up?+
Nope! Viewers can create and participate in predictions on any stream. Streamers can optionally use our dashboard to create official predictions and earn a cut.
What crypto do I need?+
PREDICT uses USDC stablecoin. Your predictions are always in stable dollars - no crypto volatility. Transaction fees are minimal.
How fast are payouts?+
Instant! Thanks to fast blockchain settlement, winnings hit your wallet the moment a prediction is resolved.
Is this legal?+
PREDICT operates as a prediction market platform. Please check your local regulations regarding prediction markets and cryptocurrency usage.
What platforms are supported?+
PREDICT works on Twitch, YouTube Live, Kick, X.com and Blaze.stream. Since it's a browser extension, it overlays on top of any streaming platform.
